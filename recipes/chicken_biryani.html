Chicken Biryani

Instant Pot Style Chicken Biryani

After testing the recipe a few times finally, I am here with tips and tricks to get the perfect biryani every time.

Here in the recipe, I explained step by step instructions so that even beginners can make this biryani. Now I make this chicken biryani in instant pot regularly.

    Ingredients:

  • Basmati Rice 2 cups
  • Green chillies 2-3
  • Sliced Onion 1 Big
  • Bay Leaves 2
  • Cloves 4
  • Star anise 1
  • Jeera 1tsp
  • Cardamom pods2
  • Black Cardamom 1
  • Cinnamon stick 1/2 inch
  • Ghee 1tbsp
  • Oil 3tbsp
  • Salt to taste
  • Chicken 500 gm
  • Yoghurt 4tbsp
  • Biryani powder4tsp

    Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl, add cleaned chicken with the ingredients mentioned above for marination and mix well. Keep it aside for at least 20-25 minutes in the fridge.
  2. Wash and soak basmati rice in enough water for 30 minutes. Once 30 minutes is done drain water from the rice and keep it aside.
  3. Place the inner pot inside the Instant Pot. Switch on the sauté mode. Once the inner pot is hot, add oil and ghee.
  4. Add shahi jeera, cloves, cinnamon, cardamom and bay leaf. Stir it.
  5. Next, add chopped onions, slit green chillies and fry until onion turns into translucent.
  6. Now add ginger garlic paste and sauté until raw smell goes out. Then add marinated chicken and sauté for few 3 to 5 minutes.
  7. Add 1 cup water and give a quick mix. Then add salt according to your taste.
  8. Cancel the sauté mode. Close the lid and keep the valve in sealing mode.
  9. Select pressure cook mode and set to high pressure for 5 minutes.
  10. Once the cooking is done, allow 10 minutes and release the pressure manually.
  11. Open the lid and give a quick stir. Drain the water from the rice and add it to the pot. Gently mix the rice without breaking the grains.
  12. Add 1 ½ cups water and give a quick mix. (NOTE: After cooking the chicken, there will be around 1 cup of liquid in the pot, for cooking 2 cups rice, add 1 ½ cups of additional water)
  13. Add coriander, mint leaves and mix well. Check the seasoning and add salt if needed.
  14. Cancel the sauté mode. Close the lid and keep the valve in sealing mode. Select pressure cook mode and set to high pressure for 5 minutes.
  15. Once the cooking is done, allow the pressure to release naturally.
